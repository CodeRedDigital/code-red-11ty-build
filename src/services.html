Web Development

Web Development covers a whole host of different things, from building websites to intranets, to single page apps to Progressive Web Apps (PWAs).

Have a look at our Ethos to get an understanding, of how we go about our development process. Whatever it is that you need help with it will be semantic, accessible, performant and carefully crafted.

Consultancy

We can provide services around all your digital platforms to gain an understanding of what you need to do to make them:

  • Faster - more performant
  • More discoverable - better SEO (Search Engine Optimisation)
  • More Accessible - easier to use for more users
  • Easy to edit, update and create content

Content Management Systems

Content Management Systems (CMSs) are there to make it easy for non-technical users to edit, update and create content. Here at Code Red we have many years experience in using CMSs and can use this knowledge and experience to help you get the most out of discovering the right CMS for you and your organisation.

Training

The importance of adoption of new tools and services comes down to understanding. Dave (our Director), with almost 30 years of experience educating, has been helping organisations with this for many a year and before moving into the digital world he was a Teacher, teaching Design and Computer Science to 11 - 18 year olds.

So if you have a new digital tool or service we can help with up-skilling your teams. We can deliver this in a number of different ways, to suit your needs, whether that be traditional classroom led to videos to tutorials to documentation.

Design

Design is not just about how something looks but also and more importantly how something works. We can help with designing the look and feel of a site/app/platform, how it works and functions and also how your team will work with and use the tools.

So if you need help with designing a website, app, portal or a process or workflow get in touch and we can work together to provide the right solution for you, your teams, and your users.
